I'm Alka

I recently completed my Ph.D. from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, in collaboration with the Chennai Mathematical Institute, India. My research focuses on complex systems, with a particular emphasis on climate change and environmental systems. I am passionate about exploring the intricate dynamics of these systems to better understand and address the pressing challenges posed by environmental transformations.
